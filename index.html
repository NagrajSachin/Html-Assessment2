Things You should Know
To get a Personal loan you must:
  • Be over 18
  • Be an Australian/New Zealand citizen or an Australian permanent resident living in Australia
  • Be eligible to work in Australia
  • Be employed or receive regular income
  • Have a good credit rating
  • Not be going through the process of bankrupty
